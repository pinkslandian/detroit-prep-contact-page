Our Goal


Detroit Public Schools Community District is committed to providing a rigorous, relevant and high-quality course of study for every student. The instructional program is designed to ensure that students graduate with the 21st century social and academic skills necessary for success in college, work, and life. The districts high standards, rigorous curriculum, and instructional programs are designed to be responsive to student needs. DPSCD also provides students with a marketplace of schools and programs that offer the best fit for their interests, including single-gender, arts and medical career paths and career technical education.
